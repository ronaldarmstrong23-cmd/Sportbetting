# Parlay Building Strategies

## Rule #1: Correlation Management
Avoid betting on correlated events. Ensure that your bets do not emphasize the same outcome, which can inflate risk.

## Rule #2: Bet Sizing
- **2-leg parlays:** Bet 1-2% of your bankroll.
- **3-leg parlays:** Bet 0.5-1% of your bankroll.
- **4-leg parlays:** Bet 0.25-0.5% of your bankroll.

## Rule #3: Odds Requirements
Only include bets with a minimum odds of -130 to ensure value.

## Rule #4: Injury Protocols
Keep updated on player injuries. Adjust your bets accordingly, as injuries can drastically affect outcomes.

## Rule #5: Good vs Bad Bet Combinations
Understand which combinations are more likely to win and which might undermine your success based on historical data.

## Rule #6: Win Rate Targets
Set realistic targets for your win rate. Ensure you understand the implications of your targets on your bankroll management.

## Rule #7: Tracking and Analysis
Thoroughly track your bets and analyze their outcomes regularly to refine your strategy and improve future performance.
